About me

I am a fun loving, creative, and dedicated Web Developer. I enjoy making new things, solving puzzles and learning new technology as I do it. How could I indulge both of these things in one place? Through Web Development, it combines creating, building, puzzle solving, and leaning new technology, what more could I ask for. I embody these things as I Develop, and compare it to writing music. It is built brick by brick and piece by piece, each part individually is cool, but together a force to make the world a better place. I one day want to combine my love for Development and music, by building or contributing to an application to connect and help people play, write, and teach music.

Some of my favorite things are music, cooking, traveling, and gaming. Music speaks to us on a different level and lets us express so much more then words can. I also love cooking, I have worked as a cook for most of my professional career. Cooking became another way to be creative by coming up with new recipes to share. Another big love of mine is traveling. I have gone on two cross contry road trips, and plan on attempting another to California in additon to visiting Europe. Lastly I am a big gamer, both board and video. I enjoy all kinds of different games from mobas and rpgs, to action adventure and shooters.
